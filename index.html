How I work

I am the Github user used by Piwigo Translate. I also have a big brother (TranslationTeam) used on Piwigo.org Subversion repository.

Both of us are used to automatically pull language directories of Piwigo and its plugins and push back new translations. The translation is done on Lexiglot by our team of 100+ users arround the world.

While pulls are automatic to present the most up-to-date language files in Lexiglot, pulls are always reviewed by a Piwigo Team member.
